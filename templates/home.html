About Jovean

Jovean is an innovative job application platform designed to bridge the gap between job seekers and employers. It streamlines the hiring process by providing a centralized space where candidates can create professional profiles, apply for jobs efficiently, and track their application status in real time. Employers can post job openings, filter candidates using intelligent search tools, and manage recruitment seamlessly.

A Site Reliability Engineer (SRE) ensures that systems are reliable, scalable, and efficient. Their main tasks include automating operational processes, monitoring system performance, managing incidents, and improving system uptime. They build tools to enhance deployment speed, maintain CI/CD pipelines, and collaborate with developers to design resilient infrastructure. SREs also perform capacity planning, root cause analysis, and establish metrics such as SLIs, SLOs, and SLAs to measure system reliability.
